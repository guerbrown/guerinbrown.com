BAGES

Biologically Accurate Gall Ecology Simulator

BAGES (Biologically Accurate Gall Ecology Simulator) is a specialized application for simulating gall ecology.

Due to GitHub Pages restrictions, we cannot host the executable file directly. However, you can access BAGES through the following methods:

  • Download: Contact me directly to obtain a copy of the BAGES application.
  • Online Demo: (Coming Soon) We're working on an online version of BAGES for easy access.

About BAGES

BAGES is designed to provide accurate simulations of gall ecology, offering insights into this fascinating aspect of biology. It's a valuable tool for researchers, students, and enthusiasts in the field of integrated biology.
